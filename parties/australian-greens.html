Current Members

There are 9 current members, consisting of 8 Senators and 1 member of the House of Representatives.

The average age of current members is 48, with the youngest being Jordon Steele-John (24) and the oldest being Janet Rice (58).

66.7% of current members are male and 33.3% are female.

On average, each member owns 1.7 properties, either solely or in partnership.

55.6% of current members have shares, and 11.1% of current members have trusts.
