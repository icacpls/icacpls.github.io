Current Members

There are 91 current members, consisting of 25 Senators and 66 members of the House of Representatives.

The average age of current members is 52, with the youngest being Patrick Gorman (33) and the oldest being Patrick Dodson (70).

53.8% of current members are male and 46.2% are female. The most common name is Anne.

On average, each member owns 2.1 properties, either solely or in partnership.

33% of current members have shares, and 17.6% of current members have trusts.

30 members own a property in Canberra and claim parliamentary travel allowance while in Canberra.
