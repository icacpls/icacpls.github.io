Current Members

There are 84 current members, consisting of 25 Senators and 59 members of the House of Representatives.

The average age of current members is 51, with the youngest being James Paterson (30) and the oldest being Ian Macdonald (72).

76.2% of current members are male and 23.8% are female. The most common name is Andrew.

On average, each member owns 2.4 properties, either solely or in partnership.

52.4% of current members have shares, and 50% of current members have trusts.

23 members own a property in Canberra and claim parliamentary travel allowance while in Canberra.
