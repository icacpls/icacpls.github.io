Current Members

There are 220 current members, consisting of 75 Senators and 145 members of the House of Representatives.

The average age of current members is 52, with the youngest being Jordon Steele-John (23) and the oldest being Derryn Hinch (74).

68.2% of current members are male and 31.8% are female. The most common name is Andrew.

On average, each member owns 2.3 properties, either solely or in partnership.

46.8% of current members have shares, and 35% of current members have trusts.
